About

Bridging Psychology & Technology

My professional journey

My path began at age 24 with a doctorate exploring how narratives shape human behavior. That research revealed a breakthrough: when we understand people through their stories rather than stereotypes, we transform how we treat them.

After helping 15,000+ young individuals as a school psychologist and witnessing patterns of marginalization, I created Talk2Alex—proving technology could deliver personalized behavioral interventions at scale.

Today at Felixa Health, I'm applying two decades of insights to online toxicity across gaming and social media platforms, transforming digital communities through psychology-grounded AI.

Exploring serious and prosocial gaming for emotional learning and resilience building.

Behavioral & Semantic Markers
